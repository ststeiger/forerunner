Jason Carlson, Founder and Chief Forerunner

Creating software solutions that make tools easy and accessible is part of Jason’s DNA.

Jason is one of Washington’s ambassadors of innovation. He brings over 20 years building and managing technology companies before creating Forerunner Software. Jason has led product innovation and engineering teams at Global Scholar, Amazon and Microsoft. Most recently, Jason was Senior Vice President of Engineering at Global Scholar. Prior to that, he was General Manager for Amazon’s web services at AWS.Amazon.com.

Jason’s 15 years leading Microsoft‘s SQL platform and services inspired him to create Forerunner Software. At Microsoft, he was the lead architect, development manager, test manager, and General Manager of one of Microsoft’s most broadly used reporting technologies. Jason holds over 20 patents and plans to keep building innovation.Jason serves on the Board of Directors for IMS Global Learning Consortium a non-profit organization that works to promote the adoption of technology in education and drive learning impacts. Jason holds a BS in Computer Science from the University of Washington.
